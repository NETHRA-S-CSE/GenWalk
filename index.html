What is GenWalk?

GenWalk is a smart footstep energy harvesting system that converts mechanical energy from footsteps into electrical power using a dynamo-based mechanism. Unlike expensive piezoelectric tiles, GenWalk offers a cost-effective, scalable, and eco-friendly solution to generate clean energy in high-footfall areas.
